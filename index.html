Our Story

Three Generations of Brooklyn Pride

Salerno Auto Body Shop has been a cornerstone of Brooklyn's automotive community since 1959. What started as a small family garage has grown into one of Brooklyn's most trusted collision and auto repair centers — now serving the third generation of loyal customers.

We combine old-school craftsmanship with modern diagnostic technology, ensuring every vehicle leaves our shop looking and performing like new. Our team of certified technicians treats every car as if it were their own.
